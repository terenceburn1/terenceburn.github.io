Biography
This is a short biography on me, Terence Burn. I was born on the French side of a small Island in the Caribbean named Saint. Martin. I spent the first 13 years of my life living on that island going to an international school named C.I.A (Caribbean International Academy and spent my free time going to the beach and such. When i was 13 I moved to Macedonia and entered into a Macedonian school named Nova International Schools. I transferred after finishing 7th grade, so i entered the school starting a new year as a middle school kid. Macedonia has been amazing for the last 3 years I have spent here and I am looking forward to more years.
